# Privacy Policy - Beyblade X Manager

**Last updated:** March 31, 2026

## Overview

Beyblade X Manager ("the App") is a free collection management app for Beyblade X enthusiasts. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

**The App does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All your collection data (parts, decks, wishlist) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

## Internet Usage

The App uses an internet connection for two purposes only:

1. **Part images**: The App loads Beyblade part images from publicly available sources (Fandom wiki) to display alongside part information. No personal data is sent in these requests.

2. **Database updates**: The App may check for an updated parts database from a GitHub repository. This is a read-only operation that downloads public data. No personal data is sent.

## Data Storage

- All user data (collection, decks, wishlist) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage directory.
- Data is NOT backed up to any cloud service.
- If you uninstall the App, all local data is permanently deleted.
- You can export your collection as a JSON file and import it on another device using the in-app settings.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App contains no advertising, in-app purchases, or social features.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
